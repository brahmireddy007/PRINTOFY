privacy policy

this page explains what information printofy collects, why it is collected, and how it is used. no weird spy behavior. just normal website stuff.

1. information we collect

printofy may collect personal information such as your name, email address, login credentials, order details, uploaded designs, and review content when you use the website.

2. how we use your information

  • to create and manage your account
  • to process and deliver your orders
  • to provide customer support
  • to improve the website and user experience
  • to display reviews submitted by users

3. password security

user passwords are stored securely in encrypted form using password hashing. printofy does not store plain text passwords.

4. uploaded content

designs, images, or text uploaded for customization may be stored for order processing purposes. users are responsible for ensuring they have rights to upload and use such content.

5. sharing of information

printofy does not sell personal information to third parties. information may only be shared when necessary for payment processing, delivery services, legal compliance, or website operation.

6. cookies and basic tracking

the website may use sessions or basic cookies to keep users logged in, maintain cart data, and improve functionality.

7. user rights

users may request correction or deletion of their account-related information, subject to legal or operational requirements.

8. data protection

printofy takes reasonable steps to protect user information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. however, no online system can guarantee complete security.

9. policy updates

this privacy policy may be updated from time to time. continued use of the website after changes means you accept the updated policy.

10. contact

if you have questions about this privacy policy, you may contact printofy through the support details available on the website.
